About Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs were ancient reptiles that lived millions of years ago. They roamed the Earth during the Mesozoic Era and came in all shapes and sizes. Some dinosaurs were gentle giants, while others were fierce predators.(Wikipedia)

facts

The word "dinosaur" means "terrible lizard". Despite the name, many dinosaurs were not actually lizards. Scientists continue to discover new species every year.(reddit)
